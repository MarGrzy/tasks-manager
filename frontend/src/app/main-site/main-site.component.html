About

Introduction

This application was created with a view to better time scheduling. This app lets you to keep your tasks, plans, ideas and deadlines in one place. Forget about pen and a sheet of paper - now you are able to write everything here and easily manage it. Major advantage of using such a service is possibility of being more organized and punctual. No more forgetting about things, just write them down in one place and come back to them whenever you want!
